Our Organisation Structure

NRIT organization structure is simple, headed by a Director who is also the lead consultant. It has three department handling specialized activities namely; Consultancy Services, GIS/ICT/Research, and Finance & Events management Department.

Short-term Consultancy Department

This department has employees on long and short term contracts based on workload. All the subject area lead experts have a minimum of a Masters Degree and more than five years experience in their area of expertise. Currently we have experts in the following areas;

  • Corporate strategic planning
  • Participatory forest management planning
  • Roadside tree planting and landscaping
  • Technical and managerial support to large scale (commercial) tree planting
  • Forest resource assessment
  • Applications of GIS and Remote Sensing
  • Climate change adaptation and mitigation
  • CDM and REDD+ project development
  • Development of Reference Level (RL) and MRV system
  • Development of customized software's for natural resources data management
  • Environmental Impact Assessment and Environmental Audit
  • Customer service and work environment surveys

Long-term Contracts/field operation department

This department supports contracts that are more than 9months implementation period since it requires expert's availability for a long period of time and sometime the work station is located in the client's offices. This department also handles logistics of all types of field work. Some activities we have the capacity to immplement include:

  • Landscapping
  • Highway tree planting
  • Implementation of large scale afforestation projects
  • Providing technical and labour services on tree nursery establishment
  • Providing technical services on establishment of nature based enterprise and bio-carbon projects

Planning & Training

This is a specialised department supporting the development of Corporate strategic plans, forest management plans and community capacity development. This Department is coordinated by the NRIT Principal Consultant who has worldwide knowlegde of forest planning having worked in the government and Kenya Forest Service in the Forest Resource Planning Department for several years as the head. Under corporate planning, we have the capacity and skills to support and conduct institutional long and short term strategic plans.
