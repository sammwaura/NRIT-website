About Us

NRIT Ltd is a Natural Resource and Information Technology consultancy firm with headquarters in Nairobi, Kenya. It is registered under the Companies Act (Cap. 486) as a limited company. It focuses on provisioning technical services to support improved management of Natural Resources using best practices. It has a pool of experts from various fields of natural resource management some on full time while others are on short notice call.

Our Mission

To offer customers quality and timely services that enhance their capacity to improve management of natural resources.

Our Vission

To be a lead consultancy firm in natural resource information and technology development.

Our Corevalues

  • Professionalism
  • Teamwork
  • Quality and Efficiency
  • Customer Care and Focus
  • Innovative
  • Integrity, Transperancy and Accountability
  • Building Partnerships

Our Strategy

To support Institutions/Organizations, communities and individuals adopt best practices ion management of natural resources using appropriate information and technology

Our Services

  • Corporate strategic planning
  • Participatory forest management planning(PFMP)
  • Roadside tree planting and landscaping
  • Technical and managerial support to large scale(commercial)treeplanting
  • Forest resource assesement
  • Application of GIS and Remote sensing
  • Climate change adaptation and mitigation
  • CDM and REDD+ project development
  • Development of customized software's for natural resources data management
  • Development of Reference Level(RL) and MRV system
  • Environmental Impact Assessment and Environmental Audit
  • Customer service and work environment surveys
